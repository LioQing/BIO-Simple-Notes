## Role of Water
- As a solvent
    - chemical reactions in cells
    - transport nutrients, gases, wastes
- As a reactant
    - photosynthesis
    - hydrolyze food
    - activate enzyme
- As a cooling agent
    - sweating
    - transpiration
- As a supporting agent
    - buoyancy
    - turgidity
- As a lubricant
    - mucus
    - synovial fluid

## Minerals
- Nitrogen
    - component of protein
    - component of nucleic acid
- Magnesium
    - component of chlorophyll
    - component biological energy ATP
- Calcium
    - component of bones and teeth, and cellwall
    - activator to control blood clotting and muscle contraction
- Iron
    - component of haemoglobin

## Carbohydrates
CHO, H:O = 2:1

Types:
- Monosaccharides
    - glucose
    - fructose
    - galactose
- Disaccharides
    - maltose
    - sucrose
    - lactose
- Polysaccharids
    - starch
    - glycogen
    - cellulose

Use:
- Energy source
    - glucose => oxidized
- Energy reserve
    - starch => storage
    - glycogen => storage
- Building component
    - cellulose => cell wall
    - dietary fibre
