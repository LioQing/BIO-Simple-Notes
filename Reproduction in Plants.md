## Types of Vegetative Propagation
- rhyzomes
	- properties
		- grow horizontally
	- examples
		- ginger
		- lotus
		- iris
		- water lily
		- grasses
- stem tubers
	- properties
		- swollen ends of lateral stems
	- examples
		- potato
		- artichokes
		- arrow head
- bulbs
	- properties
		- condensed stems 
		- overlapping fleshy scale leaves
	- examples
		- onion
		- garlic
		- daffodil
		- tulip
- corms
	- properties
		- short
		- vertical
	- examples
		- crocus
		- gladiolus
		- water chestnut
